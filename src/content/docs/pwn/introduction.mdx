Welcome to Binary Exploitation, also known as Pwn!

Pwn challenges revolve around you trying to hack a program by exploiting a vulnerability to gain control of the server.

It is notorious for being one of the hardest concept to grasp. Don't panic if you don't understand everything at first.

However, because of this exact reason, is what makes Pwn so valuable to learn.
Mastering Pwn challenges will allow you to solve challenges no one else can, giving you a safe and substantial lead.
